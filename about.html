About this Site

As of the current moment, this site is based on Project Milestones #1, #2, and #3 for the Basic CSS/HTML, Dynamic Frontend, Responsive Design project milestones. It adapts my research paper titled “The Ins and Outs of Nginx” into a multi-page website.
